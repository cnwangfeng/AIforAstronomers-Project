# Project-Level AI Use Statement

AI assistance was used in this project to draft and revise textbook prose,
generate and refactor small code examples, summarize logs and checks, and help
maintain release-QA notes.

Human review remained responsible for:

- scientific claims and formulas
- citation selection and attribution
- license and publication decisions
- data boundaries and provenance
- final wording and release readiness

The repository itself does not include student-private material, and the
release workflow did not intentionally send such material to external AI
services. Where AI assistance contributed to the repository, the output was
checked and edited by a human maintainer before release.
